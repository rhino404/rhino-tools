Ham Radio License Exam Prep

Practice all three FCC amateur radio license levels in one place. The Technician pool covers regulations, operating procedures, and electronics fundamentals (425+ questions). General Class adds HF propagation, antenna theory, and advanced operating (456+ questions). Amateur Extra covers the full advanced regulations, EMI, and theoretical concepts required for the top-tier license (706+ questions). Question pools are tagged by NCVEC topic area and updated to current FCC exam cycles.

Falconry Apprentice Exam Prep

The falconry apprentice exam is required in all U.S. states to legally practice falconry. It is typically 100 multiple-choice questions covering raptor biology, species identification, housing standards (mews design, perches), nutrition and health care, federal and state hunting regulations, and ethical hunting practices. A passing score of 80% is required in most states. Ryno Tools' 398+ question bank is tagged by subject area so you can isolate the topics where you need the most work.

CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701) Exam Prep

Practice questions aligned to the current CompTIA Security+ SY0-701 objectives: threats and vulnerabilities, cryptography, access control, network security, incident response, and governance & compliance. Security+ SY0-701 satisfies the DoD 8570/8140 baseline certification requirement for Information Assurance roles. CompTIA is expected to release SY0-801 in late 2026.
